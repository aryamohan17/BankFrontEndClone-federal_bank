CONTACT CENTER

Contact Center is your single point contact with the bank. Dial the pan India toll free number 1800 - 425 - 1199 Or 1800 - 420 - 1199 to avail the services of the Contact Center. Customers from abroad can dial 080-61991199. Executives will be available 24 x 7 to help you. They will divulge information regarding your accounts only after proper identification. For verification you will be requested to provide the following information.

For general enquiries:

Full name and Account number along with any two of the following details:

  • Communication address registered with bank records
  • Customer id
  • Last 3 transactions
For queries related to FedNet Internet Banking:

FedNet User ID, Full Name, Email ID registered with bank records and any one of the following details:

  • Date of Birth
  • Customer ID
